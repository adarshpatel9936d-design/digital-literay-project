 ## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
 
 ### DO’s (Responsible Social Media Use)
 
 Think before you post – ensure your content is appropriate and respectful
 
 Maintain a professional online presence (especially on LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.)
 
 Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings
 
 Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
 
 Be respectful and positive in comments and interactions

### DON’Ts (Avoid These Practices)
 
 Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
 
 Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
 
 Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
 
 Avoid clicking on suspicious links or scams
 
 Do not post anything that can harm your academic or professional reputation
 
 ## Conclusion

Using social media responsibly helps in building a positive digital identity and ensures safety in the online world. 
Always be mindful of your actions online as they leave a permanent digital footprint.
