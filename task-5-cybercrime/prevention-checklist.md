 ## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)
 
## General Safety Tips

 Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (avoid using the same password everywhere)
 
 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps
 
 Do not click on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp
 
 Always verify unknown calls/messages claiming to be from banks or officials
 
 Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches
 
 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts (banking, email, etc.)

 
 ## Financial / UPI Safety Tips (Important)
 
 Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
 
 Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve “collect requests” without verifying the sender
 
 Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN
 
 Always double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment
 
 
 ## If You Become a Victim
 
 Immediately report the incident on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 
 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance
 
 Inform your bank and block transactions as soon as possible
 
 ## Conclusion

Being aware and cautious while using the internet can prevent most cybercrimes. Always think before you click, share, or pay online.
